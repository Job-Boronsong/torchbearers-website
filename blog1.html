Empowering Rural Girls with Technology

In our recent Tech4Girls Workshop hosted in Yamfo, we brought together over 100 young girls from surrounding rural communities for an intensive 3-day training in digital literacy and coding. Each participant was guided through hands-on activities such as website creation, email setup, and introductory HTML & CSS lessons.

We partnered with local schools and volunteers to provide laptops, internet access, and mentorship. This program was designed to spark interest in STEM among girls and give them a platform to thrive.

Feedback from the attendees and community leaders was overwhelmingly positive, reinforcing our commitment to continue bridging the gender and digital divide in under-resourced areas.
