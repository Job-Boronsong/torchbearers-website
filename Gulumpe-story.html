A Story of Transformation in Gulumpe

"My journey hasn't been easy, but with God's help, we're making progress." – Gado Haruna

Meet Gado Haruna (pictured in white, smiling) and his disciples. For over two years, Gado has been committed to discipleship among these individuals in Gulumpe. Every Sunday, he travels 45 minutes from Buipe to meet with them. They are Konkombas from deeply spiritual and fetish backgrounds. Through Gado's discipleship, they have experienced remarkable transformations—breaking free from adultery, alcoholism, fetish practices, and smoking.

The group has successfully acquired land in the community and is trusting God for the resources to build a church. Gado’s prayer is for God to sustain and multiply their efforts.
